FAQ (Frequenty Asked Questions)

These are the most frequently asked question. Please go through it. To know more, contact below.
(contact details provided below)

Do I need to register to get started?

Yes. Registration is very simple. Login credentials are required to proceed.

Are the organizations legitimate ?

Legitimacy is one of the important factor. Each organization has to go through a clearance test.

Can I cancel my request or reschedule?

Yes, you can cancel your pickup request at any given time and reschedule as well.

Can organizations put additional data?

Yes. Apart from its details. Organizations can regularly post about their recent activities.

Can organization cancel any request?

Yes. They have the authority to cancel any received request if they are not able to fulfil it.

How to proceed after scheduling a request?

The respective organization would contact you. You can track your request status.
